About Children Global

The first and only baby gear manufacturer in the GCC and Middle East

Our Story

Children Global was founded with a vision to become the leading manufacturer of premium baby products in the Middle East. Established in Dubai's Jebel Ali Free Zone (JAFZA), we've grown to become the first and only baby gear manufacturer in the GCC region.

Our commitment to quality, safety, and innovation has made us the trusted partner for many leading American and European baby brands. We specialize in manufacturing a wide range of baby products including car seats, strollers, play yards, highchairs, swings, carriers, and nursery items – all designed and engineered to meet the highest international safety standards.

With a team of experienced professionals and state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities, Children Global continues to set the benchmark for baby product manufacturing in the region.

Our Mission

To manufacture the highest quality baby products that ensure safety, comfort, and happiness for children across the globe, while setting new standards for innovation and manufacturing excellence in the GCC region.

Our Vision

To become the global partner of choice for premium baby product manufacturing, recognized worldwide for our unwavering commitment to safety standards, innovative design, and manufacturing excellence.
