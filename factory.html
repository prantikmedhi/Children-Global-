Our Manufacturing Facility

Factory Overview

Take a tour of the first and only baby gear manufacturing facility in the GCC region

Children Global operates a world-class manufacturing facility in Dubai's Jebel Ali Free Zone (JAFZA). Our factory is equipped with the latest technology and machinery, enabling us to produce premium baby products that meet international safety standards.

As the first baby gear manufacturer in the GCC, we've invested heavily in creating a facility that can handle every aspect of the manufacturing process – from design and prototyping to testing and quality assurance. Our integrated production lines ensure efficiency while maintaining the highest quality standards.

The strategic location of our factory in JAFZA provides significant logistical advantages, allowing us to ship products globally with reduced customs duties and simplified export procedures.

Our Manufacturing Process

From concept to finished product

1

Design & Engineering

Our process begins with product design and engineering, where our experienced team creates detailed blueprints and specifications based on market research and safety requirements.

2

Prototyping

We develop working prototypes of new products to test functionality, ergonomics, and design aesthetics before moving to full production.

3

Material Sourcing

We source high-quality, safe, and durable materials from trusted suppliers, ensuring all components meet our strict quality standards.

4

Production

Our production lines employ precision manufacturing techniques and are staffed by skilled workers who combine craftsmanship with technological efficiency.

5

Quality Control

Every product undergoes rigorous quality checks at multiple stages of production, with comprehensive safety testing before final approval.

6

Packaging & Shipping

Products are carefully packaged and prepared for shipping from our JAFZA facility, taking advantage of the strategic location for global distribution.

JAFZA Location Advantages

Our manufacturing facility is strategically located in Jebel Ali Free Zone (JAFZA), one of the world's leading free zones and a key logistics hub.

  • Direct access to Jebel Ali Port, the largest port in the Middle East
  • Proximity to Al Maktoum International Airport for air freight
  • 100% foreign ownership with no corporate taxes
  • No import or export duties within the free zone
  • Streamlined customs procedures for faster shipping
  • Access to a skilled workforce
  • World-class infrastructure and utilities
  • Strategic position between Asian and European markets
