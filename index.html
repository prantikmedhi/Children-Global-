Why Children Global Factory for your products?

⸻

Because we are the first baby gear manufacturer in the GCC and Middle East with full ASTM and EN certifications. Based in JAFZA – Dubai, we deliver premium-quality baby products through OEM and ODM solutions for globally renowned brands. From concept to production, our innovation, compliance, and efficiency make us the trusted partner for baby product excellence.

Why ASTM standards?

⸻

ASTM standards are the gold benchmark for baby product safety in the U.S. and international markets. Compliance ensures our products meet the highest safety, durability, and performance criteria, giving our clients peace of mind and regulatory confidence.

Why EN standards?

⸻

EN standards represent the European Union's strictest safety requirements. Our adherence to EN certification reflects our commitment to providing safe, child-friendly, and legally approved products for the European and global markets.

Why JAFZA?

⸻

JAFZA (Jebel Ali Free Zone) is one of the world's most strategic trade hubs, offering world-class infrastructure, customs exemptions, and global logistics connectivity. Being in JAFZA gives us direct access to shipping routes, reduced costs, and faster delivery for our clients worldwide.

Why our 30+ years of knowledge matters?

⸻

Our leadership team brings over three decades of experience in baby product design, engineering, and market research. This depth of industry knowledge ensures that every product we develop is technically advanced, globally compliant, and tailored to market trends — because children's safety deserves nothing less.
