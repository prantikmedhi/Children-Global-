UAE Golden Visa for Entrepreneurs

The UAE Golden Visa offers long-term residency benefits for business owners, investors, and exceptional talents. Our team will guide you through the entire application process.

Eligibility Requirements:

  • Ownership or partnership in a UAE-based startup
  • Annual revenue of at least AED 1 million
  • Approval of business plans by relevant UAE authorities
  • Investment in specific sectors (technology, healthcare, etc.)
  • Specialized talents in priority fields may qualify

Key Benefits:

  • 10-year renewable residency
  • 100% ownership of mainland companies
  • No personal income tax
  • Full profit repatriation
  • Family sponsorship (spouse, children, parents)
  • Access to premium healthcare and education
